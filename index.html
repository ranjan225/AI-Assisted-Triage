The problem

Triage doesn't scale — and the noise is getting worse.

Disclosure programs receive more reports than analysts can keep up with, and a growing share is automated low-quality submissions.

🌊

The AI-slop flood

A rising tide of machine-generated, plausible-but-empty reports buries the genuine findings and wastes senior analyst time.

⏳

Slow, inconsistent triage

First-pass review is manual, repetitive, and varies analyst to analyst. The real issues wait in the queue behind the noise.

🔒

Cloud triage is off the table

Regulated and security-sensitive teams can't send vulnerability reports to a third-party SaaS — so they get no AI help at all.

How it works

Signal first, on your infrastructure.

AI Assisted Triage sits in front of your triage queue and gives every report a clear, explained scorecard — your team stays in control of every decision.

A report arrives

Connects to how reports already reach you. Everything runs inside your environment — nothing is sent out.

It's scored in seconds

Each report gets a triage scorecard — quality, duplicate risk, AI-likelihood, severity and more — with a short rationale for each call.

Analysts act on the signal

The noise is flagged, the real findings rise to the top, and your team makes the final call faster and more consistently.
